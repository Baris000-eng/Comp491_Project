KuClass Student Login Screen Guide

In the student login screen, please type the username, password, and email which you have used for signing up to the system to their corresponding textboxes. Then, in order to login to the system, press the Login button below the textboxes. If you forgot your password, you can click on the "Forgot Password" button which is below the "Login" button. If you have not signed up to the system yet, please firstly signup. Please make sure that the password you type to the password textbox contains at least 1 special character, 1 uppercase letter, 1 lowercase letter, and 1 digit. Please also make sure that your login password contains at least 8 characters. If you have not got an email address which is from KU Domain, you cannot login to our system. So, make sure that you are logging in to our system with an email which belongs to KU Domain. Finally, for observing the guide information about student login screen, please press "Open Student Login Guide" button.
