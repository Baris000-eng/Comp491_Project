KuClass Web App Description

In the exam weeks, students have difficulty in Ko√ß University to find a private place in school for studying. Our project is a web app that aims to help students to find vacant classrooms, reserve them, and communicate with each other about studying in them. Therefore, the empty classrooms will be used more efficiently. Buildings, classrooms, and their class schedules will be available through the app. This will enable the students to make reservations for vacant time slots to study. They can announce what class they are studying in and choose if they allow other students to enter or not. This may enable students to get together for studying on the same class/topic. Moreover; our project will be able to find the classes which have broken items/materials (ex: chair, desk, projector) in them, and will enable the necessary help for those classes to be obtained. As a result, the students can know which classes have some broken materials/items, and update their classroom preferences accordingly. Moreover, the students can update their classroom preferences also according to the number of currently studying students in the classrooms. This will help with finding suitable classrooms for students to study in.
