KuClass Admin Signup Screen Guide

Please enter your username, password, and email in the corresponding textboxes of the admin signup screen. Please do not leave any of these fields empty. You cannot signup to the system unless your email is from the KU Domain. The password you will type should include at least 8 characters. Among these characters, at least; 1 special character, 1 lowercase character, 1 digit, and 1 uppercase character should appear. Once you have entered all fields, you can press the Submit button and then proceed to the Admin Dashboard Screen.
